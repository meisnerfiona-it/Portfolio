Macroeconomic Analytics Python • APIs • Regression

Energy Price Shock & Inflation Transmission Across European Economies

Quantified how energy price shocks translate into inflation across major EU economies, enabling clearer risk assessment and policy response under volatile market conditions.

Approach
  • Engineered a harmonized, multi-country time series dataset from Eurostat and ECB APIs, resolving frequency mismatches, missing data, and structural inconsistencies.
  • Developed a clean analytical pipeline to align inflation (HICP) with energy price indices across Germany, France, Italy, and Spain.
  • Quantified the strength and timing of energy-to-inflation transmission using correlation and regression analysis, isolating effects such as the 2021–2022 energy shock.
  • Segmented analysis across macroeconomic regimes (pre-Green Deal and energy crisis) to evaluate shifts in inflation dynamics and persistence.
  • Benchmarked cross-country volatility and resilience, identifying Italy as highly reactive to shocks and France as comparatively stable.
Business impact: Translates complex macroeconomic data into actionable insight—highlighting how energy-driven inflation risk varies across economies, enabling more informed policy evaluation, market analysis, and strategic decision-making under volatile conditions.

Residential Property Valuation Modeling

This project builds a machine learning pipeline for residential property valuation using structured preprocessing, feature engineering, and gradient boosting. The objective is to improve pricing accuracy and demonstrate how predictive analytics can support valuation, lending, and market intelligence decisions.

Approach
  • Cleaned and prepared housing data with robust missing-value handling for categorical and numerical features.
  • Engineered features such as total square footage, porch area, weighted bathroom count, and usable outdoor space.
  • Applied one-hot encoding and aligned train/test datasets to preserve a consistent modeling structure.
  • Trained an XGBoost regressor with early stopping and evaluated performance using RMSE on log-transformed prices.
Business impact: Demonstrates how predictive modeling can support more consistent property valuation, stronger pricing decisions, and improved analytical workflows in real estate or lending contexts.
